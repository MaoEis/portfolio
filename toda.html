Mobile App for sustainable clothes sharing.

Summary

Project Brief

This individual assignment, issued by Thomas More, challenged us to create a digital solution that promotes sustainability within the fashion industry. The project required in-depth research on sustainable fashion practices, the development of a concept based on our findings, and the design and implementation of a functional application. We were asked to build at least five screens, visually present the concept through pitch materials and pamphlets, and convincingly communicate its value through a short verbal pitch.

Solution

Toda is a peer-to-peer clothing exchange app designed to make sustainable fashion easy and social. Instead of buying new outfits for every special occasion, users can share pieces from their existing wardrobe with friends, keeping their style fresh without contributing to overconsumption. The platform encourages a more mindful approach to fashion by reducing waste, extending the life cycle of clothing, and making it easier to access variety without constant purchasing. Through intuitive design and a community-driven model, Toda supports a shift toward more responsible and conscious fashion habits.

Toda

Toda, an exciting new app that makes fashion more sustainable by allowing you to share your special clothing with friends. Upload a photo of your item, add a description and choose who can see and borrow it.

Problem:

In a world of fast fashion, we buy expensive outfits for special events but only wear them once. We love having something new for each occasion, yet we hang onto clothes we’re attached to but don’t really wear anymore.

Solution:

Instead of buying new outfits you'll only wear once, you can easily share your existing clothing with friends through Toda. This way, you keep your wardrobe fresh, reduce waste, and make fashion more sustainable.

Assignment

The assignment was given by Thomas More. We were individually asked to create an application that can help boost sustainability in fashion.

In this assignment we were asked to do our research on sustainable fashion, create a solution based up on the research. We had to design a landingspage and the application. Further we were asked to make paflets and a selling pitch.
